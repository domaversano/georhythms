Georhythm



What is this?

This system allows you to explore georhythms.

Select a georhythm from the grid to enlarge it on the right of the panel.

Below the georhythms is a piano roll that allows you to view the rhythm horizontally.

Time cycle refers to how many matras (units of musical time) are in a cycle. A fuller explanation of time cycles and matras can be found in this article.

Play/stop toggle animates the large georhythm and sequencer.

Rhythms are the numbers, and therefore shapes, that divide the time cycle.

This currently makes no sound and is intended for visualisation.


What are georhythms?

Polyrhythm refers to when two or more rhythms occur simultaneously. Yet, this definition encompasses virtually all music.

A Georhythm is a specific kind of polyrhythm, where two or more rhythms relate to one another through simple ratios. The individual rhythms are evenly spaced and, therefore, can be represented with simple shapes.
